Bullet

Bullets are designed to be mounted outdoors in full weather, however Amazon reviews state they do not do well in a fully exposed locations. Suggestion is to mount under eves of a house, on a balcony etc, but avoid direct weather. Some waterproofing with tape might help prevent water intrusion.

Models

Bullets have external antenna connections.

Model Frequency Ant type Gain Compatible
BM2HP 2.4 GHz Ant dependant Ant dependant Yes
BM2-Ti 2.4 GHz Ant dependant Ant dependant Yes
BM5HP 5 GHz Ant dependant Ant dependant Yes
BM5-Ti 5 GHz Ant dependant Ant dependant Yes
